デンマークの首都コペンハーゲンを拠点に、現地の肥沃な実験音楽シーンの現行最新ドキュメントとも言える意欲的なリリースを続けるレーベルSensorisk Verden。
レーベル主要アーティストのVid EddaとSoft Itemsが今回初来日、4/23東京@forestlimit、4/24京都@「外」、4/26大阪@ペフにてレーベル・ショーケースを行います。

Sensorisk Verden is a Copenhagen based label and curation platform focusing on experimental electronic music. Situated in the print- and tape dubbing workshop KLD Repro the label finds it self in a hub of books, design, music and visual art.
Sensorisk Verden has presented exhibitions, performance nights, alongside music releases on various medias. Touring with Vid Edda and Soft Items SVmusic is presented for the first time in Japan. Looking forward to meet you.
